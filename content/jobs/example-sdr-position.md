## Description

ReachGTM is seeking an energetic and motivated Sales Development Representative (SDR) to join our growing sales team. In this role, you'll be the first point of contact for potential clients, helping businesses discover how our global talent solutions can accelerate their growth.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct outbound prospecting to identify and qualify new business opportunities
- Engage with prospects via phone, email, and LinkedIn to understand their hiring and outsourcing needs
- Schedule qualified meetings for Account Executives
- Maintain accurate records in CRM system
- Collaborate with marketing team on campaign effectiveness
- Achieve and exceed monthly quotas for qualified opportunities
- Research target accounts and personalize outreach strategies
- Participate in team meetings and training sessions

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience
- 0-2 years of experience in sales, customer service, or related field
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
- Self-motivated with a results-driven approach
- Comfortable with technology and quick to learn new tools
- Ability to handle rejection and maintain positive attitude
- Experience with CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot) is a plus
